# Local LLM Licenses (Phase 1)

This document lists the local models actually used in the Phase 1 experiments and their licensing status.

## Models Included in Phase 1

### ✅ Mistral 7B Instruct (`mistral:7b-instruct`)
- **License:** Apache 2.0  
- **Status:** Fully open—research, commercial use, modification, and redistribution permitted.  
- **Source:** Mistral AI (Hugging Face / Ollama)  
- **Notes:** Apache 2.0 explicitly allows publication of derivative analyses.

---

### ✅ Gemma 2B Instruct (`gemma:2b-instruct`)
- **License:** Gemma Terms of Use (research-friendly, Apache-compatible)  
- **Status:** Open for research with attribution and compliance with Google’s usage policy.  
- **Source:** Google DeepMind (Hugging Face / Ollama)  
- **Notes:** Permits non-commercial research; workshop publication acceptable with attribution.

---

### ✅ Phi-3 Mini (`phi3:mini`)
- **License:** MIT  
- **Status:** Fully open—use, modify, redistribute, and publish derivative work.  
- **Source:** Microsoft (Hugging Face / Ollama)  
- **Notes:** Microsoft encourages research/educational use; no restrictions beyond MIT.

---

### ✅ Llama 3 8B Instruct (`llama3:8b`)
- **License:** Llama 3 Community License (Meta AI)  
- **Status:** Open for research and many commercial use cases, subject to license terms.  
- **Source:** Meta AI (Hugging Face / Ollama)  
- **Notes:** Requires adherence to community-license terms (e.g., no misuse, attribution).

---

### ✅ Orca-Mini 7B (`orca-mini:7b`)
- **License:** Apache 2.0 (via original Orca research release)  
- **Status:** Fully open for research and commercial use.  
- **Source:** Microsoft / community maintainers (Hugging Face / Ollama)  
- **Notes:** Derived from the Microsoft Orca project; Apache 2.0 applies to the weights used here.

---

## Summary

All Phase 1 models are released under permissive or clearly documented research licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0, Gemma ToU, Llama Community License). No additional usage restrictions impact this study.
